# Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the browser extension "Novel Bookmark Upsert" (the "Extension"). The Extension is distributed via the Microsoft Edge Add-ons store and runs locally in the user's browser.

**Overview**

The Extension helps users add or replace a bookmark in the browser's Bookmarks Bar for the current page. The Extension does not collect, transmit, or sell personal data to third parties.

**Data the Extension accesses or modifies**

- Bookmarks: The Extension uses the browser `bookmarks` permission to create, update, or replace bookmarks in the user's browser. Bookmarks are stored locally in the browser's bookmark storage and are not uploaded by the Extension.
- Current tab information: The Extension uses the `activeTab` permission to access the URL and title of the active tab so it can create a bookmark for that page. This data is used only to create or update the bookmark and is not sent anywhere.

**No telemetry or analytics**

The Extension does not include analytics, telemetry, crash reporting, or any third-party tracking libraries. No usage data or diagnostics are collected or transmitted.

**Data sharing and third parties**

The Extension does not share personal information with third parties. If you export or otherwise share your browser bookmarks yourself, that action is separate from the Extension and controlled by your browser.

**Security**

All data the Extension reads or modifies (bookmarks and the active tab metadata) is stored in the user's browser profile under the browser's control. The Extension does not maintain external servers or databases.

**Changes to this policy**

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If material changes occur, the effective date above will be updated.

**Contact**

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how the Extension works, please open an issue in this project's repository or consult the contact information provided in the extension listing on the Edge Add-ons page.
